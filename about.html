About Us

Recruitment and Outsourcing Specialists

Founded in 2014, MEED Consultants have built a solid reputation providing high-quality candidates and excellent customer service, delivered by a motivated team of experienced recruitment consultants. We offer staffing solutions across all medical sectors, supplying Nurses, Doctors, Allied Health Professionals (AHP) and Health Science Staff (HSS). Working in partnership with our clients, MEED Consultants always endeavour to understand specific requirements, enabling us to deliver bespoke recruitment solutions.

MEED Consultants have developed a business model that is built on providing the highest quality of compliant and skilled staff to clients. With focused customer service and a personable, professional approach, we endeavour to always meet your needs.
